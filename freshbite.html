Problem

Lack of Variety: Most delivery services offer limited options for healthy food, resulting in a lack of interest from customers.

Lack of Nutritional Information: Many users express the need for clear and accessible nutritional information about dishes, especially for those seeking healthy options.

[PERSONAS]

Anna

26 y.o.

Marketing Consultant

Office Worker
Single
New York

Anna is a busy professional who works as a marketing consultant at a medium-sized company. She is very committed to her work and often works long hours, which leaves her with little time to dedicate to preparing healthy meals at home. Anna highly values her health and well-being and is constantly looking for ways to maintain a balanced and nutritious diet despite her hectic schedule.

Challenges:

1. Lack of Time: Anna often struggles to find enough time to prepare healthy meals at home due to her busy work schedule.

2. Need for Healthy Eating: As a health-conscious individual, Anna prefers food options that are nutritious and aligned with her wellness goals.

3. Convenience and Accessibility: Due to her lack of time, Ana seeks convenient and accessible solutions for her healthy eating needs without compromising the quality of the food.

Goals:

1. Find a convenient way to maintain a healthy diet, even amidst her busy work schedule.

2. Enjoy fresh, nutritious, and tasty food options that contribute to her health and well-being.

3. Simplify the process of purchasing and receiving healthy food, saving time and effort in her daily routine.
